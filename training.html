Training

 One-on-One Personal Training: Come in for an initial assessment to determine your needs based on your goals,
health history, exercise experience, etc. After the assessment I will put you on an exercise program that will be
designed for your individual needs. Need help with your dietary habits? I will go over general recommendations
and ways to make improvements in your daily nutrition as every good exercise program needs to be accompanied
by proper eating habits for optimal success. The option of individual training allows you to get undivided attention
and instruction from a personal trainer, while also creating superior accountability to your program.
Contact Shoot4Results today if you are interested in learning more about my personal training!

 I also perform dual training which resembles one on one training in that you will come in for an
initial assessment to determine your needs based on your goals, health history, exercise experience,
etc. After the assessment I will put you on an exercise program that will be designed for your
individual needs. But I understand not everyone can afford the cost of individual personal training.
The "dual" option allows you to work out with another client which helps decrease your cost, all
while engaging you with a workout partner during your sessions. You can sign-up with someone
you know, or get put into any of the regular dual training openings that are available at that time.
This option of training allows you to get some one on one attention and instruction from a personal
trainer, while sharing the space with a client that is as motivated as you are.
